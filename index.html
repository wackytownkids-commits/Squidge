do water squishies leak?
Not if you treat them normal. Don't poke them with sharp things. If one ever does leak on you within a week, I'll replace it for free.

04
how do I pay?
Cash at pickup, or Venmo / Zelle / Cash App for shipping. I'll send you the handle in DM once your ticket is confirmed. Always paid before shipping.

05
can I order a bunch for a party?
Yes — over 10 is a custom order. Submit a ticket with details in the notes at least a week ahead and I'll do $4 each instead of $5.

06
how long do they last?
If you keep them out of the sun and don't squeeze them with sharp nails, slime ones last 6+ months. Water ones last about a year before the texture starts to change.
